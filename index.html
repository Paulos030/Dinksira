Our Story

Dinksira was born from a shared vision between Saron Gezahegn and Fikir Mesfin — to preserve Ethiopia's rich textile heritage while embracing sustainable practices. Every piece is handcrafted using traditional weaving techniques passed down through generations.

We partner with local artisans in Addis Ababa, using recycled leather to reduce waste and celebrate circular design. Our pieces are more than fashion; they are stories of culture, female leadership, and respect for the earth.
