Our Journey

DINKSIRA
HANDMADE BY ETHIOPIANS

Founded by Saron Gezahegn and Fikir Mesfin, Dinksira is an Ethiopian brand that honors ancestral weaving techniques while embracing sustainable innovation. Every shoe, every garment is handcrafted using recycled leather and traditional Ethiopian textiles.

We believe in circular design, artisanal dignity, and preserving cultural heritage — one stitch at a time.
