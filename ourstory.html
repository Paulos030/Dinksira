Handmade by Ethiopians

DINKSIRA was born from a deep respect for Ethiopian craftsmanship — a tradition of hand-weaving, leatherworking, and textile art that has been passed down through generations. We believe that clothing is more than fabric; it is identity, memory, and story.

Every piece in our collection is handcrafted by artisans in Addis Ababa and the surrounding rural communities. We work directly with master weavers, leather artisans, and dyers who use techniques that have remained unchanged for centuries.

"We don't follow seasons. We follow instinct. Every stitch tells a story."

Slow Fashion, Eternal Values

In a world of fast fashion, DINKSIRA stands for the opposite: slow, intentional, and ethical production. Our materials are locally sourced — Ethiopian cotton, highland wool, and vegetable-tanned leathers. Each garment is made to last a lifetime, gaining character with age.

We are proud to support local communities, provide fair wages, and preserve endangered craft techniques. When you wear DINKSIRA, you carry a piece of Ethiopian heritage with you.
